Food Information

Healthy Eating Tips

  • 🍎 Eat a variety of fruits and vegetables.
  • 🌾 Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • ⚖️ Limit added sugars and saturated fats.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Nutritional Facts

Here are some key nutritional facts about common ingredients:

  • 🥕 Carrots: Rich in beta-carotene, fiber, and vitamin K.
  • 🌿 Green Peas: High in protein, fiber, and vitamins A and C.
  • 🌽 Corn Flour: A good source of carbohydrates and fiber.
  • 🧂 Salt: Essential in small amounts, but excessive intake can lead to health issues.
